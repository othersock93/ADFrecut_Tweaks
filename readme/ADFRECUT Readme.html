Intro ADF Re-Cut is a community continuation of the much-beloved Australian Defense Force Uncut (ADFU) Mod. We aim to achieve the original mod goal of "re-creating as authentically as possible the uniforms, vehicles and weapons pertinent to the ADF."

You can contact the ADF Re-Cut team via these platforms.
Discord: ADF Re-Cut
1. Subscribe to the mod on Steam here
2. Add the mod and its dependancies in your Arma 3 launcher
3. Launch Arma 3!
4. Once your game is loaded you may want to re-bind ADF Re-Cut/ADFU's new actions, these can be found in Configure>>Controls>>Configure Addons
